Overview

This proposal outlines the scope, timeline, and investment for designing and developing a professional website for Diligent Auto Detailing & Maintenance. The goal is a high-converting, credibility-first website that drives bookings, communicates professionalism, and positions Diligent Auto as the go-to mobile detailing service across Brampton and Mississauga.

Scope of Work

What We're Building

A fully custom, mobile-optimised website built to convert visitors into booked clients — no templates, no shortcuts.

01
Homepage
Hero section with tagline, trust signals, and a primary CTA to book.
02

Full breakdown of Fresh & Clean, Diligent Pro, and Headlight Restoration with compact vs. large car pricing and a special requests section.

Integrated form for standard bookings. Phone number prominently featured for fleet and special enquiries.
04

Visual proof of quality using your supplied photos in a slider or grid layout.

On-page SEO targeting mobile detailing keywords in Brampton & Mississauga.
08
Social & Mobile Optimisation
Instagram link, click-to-call, and a fully responsive layout built for mobile-first visitors.
